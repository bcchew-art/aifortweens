What is OpenClaw?

An open-source AI assistant that runs on your own computer and listens through everyday apps like WhatsApp. It has memory, can browse the web, manage files, and be taught new tricks — called Skills. Think of it as your child's own personal Jarvis.

What is Vibe Coding?

One of the most exciting skills your child will learn today. Here's how it compares to traditional coding:

Traditional Coding

  • 📖Learn syntax & logic first
  • ⏳Years to be productive
  • ✍️You write every line
  • 🐛One mistake breaks it
  • 🚧Barrier: technical knowledge

Vibe Coding

  • 💬Describe in plain English
  • ⚡Productive from day one
  • 🤖AI writes, you direct
  • 🔁Tell AI what's wrong, it fixes
  • 🎯Barrier: knowing what you want
💡 Key message: Vibe coding doesn't replace learning to code — but your child can build real things before they ever learn a single line of syntax. The skill being taught is thinking clearly and communicating precisely — valuable for life, not just tech.

AI Usage — How It Works

Every message sent to the agent uses AI credits (tokens). We recommend a two-stage approach based on your child's progress:

✅ Stage 1 — Start Here
OpenRouter — $20 Credit
Pay-as-you-go, no expiry. Choose any AI model. No credit = no response — simple and transparent. Ideal while usage habits are still unknown. With responsible prompting, $20 should last well beyond the session.
⏳ Stage 2 — When Ready
GPT Plus $30/mo + OpenRouter as Fallback
Once your child understands token responsibility, upgrade to GPT Plus for a fixed monthly cost. Use OpenRouter as a fallback when the weekly limit is reached. Only move here when you're confident in their prompting habits.
⚠️ Why not GPT Plus from day one? GPT Plus has an unpredictable weekly usage limit. Hitting the limit mid-session means the agent goes silent for the rest of the week — with no warning. Frustrating for kids, confusing for parents.

Before You Arrive

One thing to prepare

🦞Think of a name for your agent — and why that name. The "why" helps shape its persona. Is it serious or playful? Formal or friendly? A strong character makes the agent feel truly yours.
💻Bringing an old laptop? Great — we can set it up on the spot. But make sure it has no sensitive personal data stored on it. Once OpenClaw is installed, the agent will have access to everything on that machine — files, folders, all of it.
💡

Tips for Your Child

🎯Be specific — the clearer the instruction, the better the result
🔁If it gets something wrong, just tell it what to fix
💸Shorter, clearer prompts use fewer tokens — say more with less
🔬Experiment freely — you can't break it by asking questions

For Parents — After the Session

Talk about it with your child

  • →What was the coolest thing they made the agent do?
  • →Can they explain "vibe coding" in their own words?
  • →What Skill would they build next if they had their own setup?

Safety reminders

  • ⚠Always supervise setup — agent has access to files & internet
  • ⚠Never share passwords or personal info with any AI
  • ⚠AI can make mistakes — double-check important answers
  • ⚠Monitor credit usage — set a monthly top-up budget

⚠️ Warning: Downloading Skills

  • 🚫ClawHub has unlimited community-built skills to download
  • 🚫But OpenClaw is open source — anyone can publish a skill
  • 🚫A malicious skill could give a hacker full access to your machine
  • ✓If you want a skill you found online — ask your agent to build it instead. Same result, zero risk.
🔴 Treat downloading Skills like installing an app from an unknown developer. When in doubt — don't install it.
