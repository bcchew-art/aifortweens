🤖 What is OpenClaw?

A free AI assistant that lives on your own computer and chats through WhatsApp. It remembers your child, can search the web, and can be taught new abilities — called Skills. Think of it as your child's own personal Jarvis.

🎮 What is Vibe Coding?

One of the most exciting things your child will learn today:

Traditional Coding

  • 📖Learn syntax first
  • ⏳Years to be productive
  • ✍️You write every line
  • 🐛One mistake breaks it
  • 🚧Barrier: tech knowledge

Vibe Coding ✨

  • 💬Describe in English
  • ⚡Productive day one
  • 🤖AI writes, you direct
  • 🔁Tell AI what's wrong
  • 🎯Barrier: clear thinking
💡 Key idea: Your child can build real things before learning a single line of code. The skill being taught is thinking clearly and communicating well — useful for life, not just tech.

⚡ AI Usage — What It Costs & How to Manage It

Every message to the agent uses a small amount of AI credit. Think of it like a prepaid data plan. Here's how we suggest starting:

✅ Stage 1 — Start Here
Prepaid AI Credits — $20
Load $20 of credit. No monthly fee, no expiry. Top up when it runs out. Simple, transparent, and teaches kids to be thoughtful. $20 should last well beyond today.
⏳ Stage 2 — When Ready
ChatGPT Plus $30/mo + Prepaid as Backup
Once your child has good habits, switch to a flat monthly plan. Keep prepaid as backup for when the monthly limit runs out.
⚠️ Why not ChatGPT Plus from day one? It has an unpredictable weekly cap. When hit, the agent goes completely silent for the rest of the week — with no warning.

✏️ Before You Arrive
One thing to prepare
🤖Think of a name for your agent — and why that name. The "why" shapes its persona. Is it serious or playful? Formal or friendly? A strong character makes it feel truly yours.
💻Bringing an old laptop? Great — we can set it up on the spot. But make sure it has no sensitive personal data. Once OpenClaw is installed, the agent has access to everything on that machine.
💡 Tips for Your Child
🎯Be specific — the clearer the instruction, the better the result
🔁If it gets something wrong, just tell it what to fix
💸Shorter, clearer messages use less credit — say more with fewer words
🔬Experiment freely — you can't break it by asking questions
👨‍👩‍👧 For Parents — After the Session
Talk about it with your child
  • →What was the coolest thing they made the agent do?
  • →Can they explain "vibe coding" in their own words?
  • →What Skill would they build next if they had their own setup?
Safety reminders
  • ⚠Always supervise setup — agent has access to files & internet
  • ⚠Never share passwords or personal info with any AI
  • ⚠AI can make mistakes — double-check important answers
  • ⚠Monitor credit usage — set a monthly top-up budget
⚠️ Warning: Downloading Skills
  • 🚫OpenClaw is open source — anyone can publish a Skill
  • 🚫A malicious skill could give a hacker full access to your machine
  • ✓If you want a skill online — ask your agent to build it instead. Same result, zero risk.
🔴 Treat downloading Skills like installing an app from an unknown developer. When in doubt — don't install it.
